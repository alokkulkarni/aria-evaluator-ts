## Notes

### SAFE note terms

Recommended seed SAFE assumptions for planning:

- **Instrument:** post-money SAFE
- **Valuation cap:** **$8M**
- **Discount:** **none**
- **MFN:** **included**
- **Conversion:** typically converts in the next priced equity financing

### ESOP guidance

- Keep the early seed option pool lean until key hires are clearer
- A larger **10–15% ESOP** is commonly established or refreshed at **Series A**
- If ARIA expects to hire aggressively before Series A, consider expanding the pool earlier and modeling the resulting founder dilution up front

### Pro-rata rights

- Seed investors commonly ask for **pro-rata rights** so they can maintain ownership in the next round
- This is usually acceptable for a seed SAFE / seed investor set, especially if rights are limited to major investors

### Cap table management

Recommended tools:

- **Carta**
- **Pulley**

Both are appropriate for:

- founder stock issuance
- SAFE tracking
- board / investor reporting
- option grants and vesting administration

## Suggested founder equity hygiene checklist

- Document founder vesting (typically 4 years with 1-year cliff)
- File **83(b)** elections on time where applicable
- Keep signed IP assignment agreements on file for all founders and contractors
- Track every issuance, transfer restriction, and board approval from day one

# Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement

> **Disclaimer:** This is a template only. Consult a qualified attorney before execution.

## INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY ASSIGNMENT AGREEMENT

This Intellectual Property Assignment Agreement (the **"Agreement"**) is entered into between **[Founder Name]** (the **"Assignor"**) and **[Company Name]** (the **"Company"**), effective as of **[Effective Date]**.

### 1. Assignment of Intellectual Property

For good and valuable consideration, including **[X shares of Common Stock / $1.00]**, receipt and sufficiency of which are acknowledged, Assignor hereby irrevocably assigns, transfers, and conveys to the Company all of Assignor's right, title, and interest, worldwide, in and to all intellectual property and related rights created, authored, conceived, reduced to practice, or otherwise owned or controlled by Assignor that relate to the Company's business.

### 2. Covered Intellectual Property

The assigned intellectual property includes, without limitation:

- source code
- object code
- algorithms
- models and evaluation logic
- documentation
- specifications
- product designs
- workflows
- prompts
- datasets
- training materials
- trade secrets
- inventions
- discoveries
- improvements
- know-how
- trademarks, logos, and branding elements to the extent transferable

### 3. ARIA Evaluator-Specific Assets Included

Without limiting the scope of this Agreement, the assignment specifically includes:

- the **ARIA Evaluator** platform
- the **ariaeval.io** website
- all infrastructure code
- all evaluation scenarios
- all compliance documentation
- all security policies
- all dashboards, reports, user flows, and interface designs
- all deployment, automation, and configuration materials

### 4. Irrevocable Worldwide Assignment

This assignment is **irrevocable**, **worldwide**, and includes all rights to use, reproduce, modify, distribute, display, license, enforce, commercialize, and otherwise exploit the assigned intellectual property in any medium now known or later developed.

### 5. Further Assurances

Assignor agrees to execute such additional documents and take such further actions as may reasonably be requested by the Company to evidence, perfect, register, maintain, or enforce the Company's rights in the assigned intellectual property.

### 6. Moral Rights Waiver

To the extent permitted by applicable law, Assignor waives and agrees not to assert any moral rights, droit moral, or similar rights in the assigned intellectual property against the Company or its successors, assigns, licensees, or customers.

### 7. Representations and Warranties

Assignor represents and warrants that:

- Assignor has full authority to enter into this Agreement
- Assignor owns or controls the assigned intellectual property or has the legal right to assign it
- the assigned intellectual property does not knowingly infringe, misappropriate, or violate any third-party intellectual property or other rights
- Assignor has not granted any rights inconsistent with this Agreement

### 8. Prior Inventions Schedule

Any pre-existing intellectual property not assigned under this Agreement must be listed on **Schedule A (Prior Inventions)** attached to this Agreement. Any intellectual property not listed on Schedule A will be deemed included in the assignment to the fullest extent permitted by law.

### 9. Confidentiality

Assignor shall keep confidential all non-public information relating to the Company's business, technology, customers, security, operations, and products. These confidentiality obligations survive termination of service, employment, or other relationship with the Company.

### 10. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of **[Delaware / England and Wales / applicable jurisdiction]**, without regard to conflicts of law principles.

### 11. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior discussions or understandings relating to such subject matter.

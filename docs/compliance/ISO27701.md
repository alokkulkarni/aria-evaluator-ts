# ISO 27701 Compliance — ARIA Evaluator

> **Framework**: ISO/IEC 27701:2019 — Privacy Information Management System (PIMS)  
> **Target**: Q1 2027 (after ISO 27001 certification)  
> **Prerequisite**: ISO 27001:2022 certification  
> **Scope**: Full platform — privacy-specific extension of ISMS

---

## What is ISO 27701?

ISO 27701 is an extension to ISO 27001 that adds privacy-specific requirements. It provides a framework for managing personal information (PII) and maps directly to GDPR and CCPA obligations. Certification demonstrates a mature, audited privacy management system.

**Why we need it**: Provides a certifiable privacy framework that complements our GDPR compliance and gives enterprise customers an independent assurance of our privacy practices.

## Certification Process

1. **Requires ISO 27001 as prerequisite** — can be pursued as an integrated audit
2. **Gap analysis** against ISO 27701 additional controls (2–3 weeks)
3. **PIMS documentation** — Privacy-specific policies and procedures (3–4 weeks)
4. **Integrated audit** with ISO 27001 (adds ~1 week to ISO 27001 audit)
5. **Certification issued** — Same 3-year cycle as ISO 27001

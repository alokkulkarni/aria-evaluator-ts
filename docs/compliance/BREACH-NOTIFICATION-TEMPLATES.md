# Breach Notification Templates

- **Effective date:** June 2026
- **Version:** 1.0
- **Owner:** Founder / Security Lead
- **Related requirement:** GDPR Articles 33 and 34

## Purpose

This document provides practical templates for ARIA Evaluator to use when a personal data breach requires notification to a supervisory authority and/or affected data subjects.

## Severity Assessment Matrix

| Scenario | Notify Supervisory Authority? | Notify Data Subjects? | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Breach unlikely to result in risk to rights and freedoms | No | No | Document internally and retain incident record |
| Breach likely to result in a risk to rights and freedoms | Yes | Usually no, unless high risk | Notify authority within 72 hours of awareness |
| Breach likely to result in a high risk to rights and freedoms | Yes | Yes | Notify authority within 72 hours and data subjects without undue delay |

## Template B: Notification to Data Subjects (GDPR Art. 34)

**When to use:** Use clear and plain language when the breach is likely to result in a high risk to affected individuals.

### Suggested subject line

`Important notice about your ARIA Evaluator data`

### Template

Hello,

We are writing to let you know about a security incident involving ARIA Evaluator.

#### What happened

On **[date]**, we identified **[brief description of incident]**. We contained the issue and started an investigation immediately.

#### What data was affected

The information involved may have included **[describe affected data categories in plain language]**.

#### What we are doing

We have taken the following steps:

- **[containment step]**
- **[remediation step]**
- **[additional monitoring or protection step]**

We are also reviewing our systems and controls to reduce the chance of this happening again.

#### What you should do

We recommend that you:

- **[recommended action, e.g. reset password]**
- **[recommended action, e.g. watch for suspicious emails]**
- **[recommended action, if applicable]**

#### Contact information

If you have questions, please contact us at **privacy@ariaeval.io** or **[support contact]**.

We are sorry this happened and will provide updates if we learn additional information that is relevant to you.

Sincerely,  
**AriaEval Ltd / ARIA Evaluator**
